By Air

If you will be traveling by air, the Reno-Tahoe airport is the closest and has shuttles to Homewood. If you don't mind a little longer drive through the mountains, Sacramento can be more affordable.

  • Reno-Tahoe International Airport — 1 hr drive to Homewood.
  • Sacramento International Airport — 2 hr drive to Homewood.
  • Oakland International Airport — 3 hr drive to Homewood.
  • San Francisco International Airport — 3.5 hr drive to Homewood.

Things to Do

Tahoe is full of things to do! Here is a list of our favorite hikes, activities, and restaurants.

Hiking

  • Eagle Rock: 20 minute hike with an amazing panoramic view of Tahoe.
  • Sugar Pine Point State Park - Lily Pond: 5 miles up to an alpine lily pond
  • List of Tahoe Hikes: mix of easy to strenuous hikes.

Outdoor Rentals

  • Biking
  • Kayaking & Paddling Boarding
  • Boat & Jet Skis: SWA Watersports or Obexer's Boat Company
